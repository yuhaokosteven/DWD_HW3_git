Under the City

INTRODUCTION

Under the City is an augmented reality applications that connects real world and virtual world. Imaging there is a crazy minion world underground! In this world, minions are crazy playing around with their world. After we went to the Time Square in New York City and looked around the buildings to see what we can do with them, we came up a great idea of destroy the floor, buildings and screens by making holes on them. Once the hole came up user will see an other totally different world in the other side of the hole. We want to include the gesture detection in the ARKit, and let users to use gesture to create the hole that connects to another world. The hole will be a portal of the reality and fantasy world, and we will make a series of fantasy world for people to play with.
